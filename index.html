Meet the team

One trusted team. End to end.

Massive Integrated Homes is a Brisbane Southside team built around home theatre fit-outs, smart home automation, electrical work, security and connectivity — all the trades that have to work together to make a modern home actually work. Based in Springfield with years of hands-on experience installing media rooms, mounting AV gear, automating lighting and tying it all together properly. We started Massive Integrated Homes because the best home projects happen when these jobs are handled by one team, not pieced together by strangers who've never met.

We turn up when we say we will, we explain things in plain English, and we leave your place cleaner than we found it. Whether it's a new powerpoint, a full smart home rollout, or a media room fit-out that ties it all together — you get the same team working the job from start to finish.
